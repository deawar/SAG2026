5. Auctions & Bidding

5.1 Auction Creation

Only Teachers and School Administrators may create Auctions. All Auctions are associated with a registered School and are subject to School Administrator approval before going live.

5.2 Bidding Rules

  • Bids are legally binding offers. Placing a bid constitutes a commitment to purchase the Artwork if you are the winning bidder.
  • All bids are final once placed. You may not retract a bid except where required by law.
  • The highest bid at auction close wins. In the event of a tie, the earliest bid prevails.
  • Real-time bid updates are delivered via WebSocket. SAG is not responsible for network delays.
  • SAG reserves the right to cancel or void any bid placed in violation of these Terms or as a result of technical error.

5.3 Reserve Prices

Teachers may set reserve (minimum) prices on Artwork. If no bid meets or exceeds the reserve price, no sale occurs and no payment is charged.

5.4 Auction Cancellation

SAG and School Administrators reserve the right to cancel an Auction at any time. If an Auction is cancelled after bids have been placed, all bidders will be notified and no payments will be processed.
